My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. MS Dhoni: The Untold Story

    Year Released: 2016

    Main Actors: Sushant Singh Rajput, Kiara Advani, Anupam Kher

    Description: This biographical sports drama chronicles the life of Mahendra Singh Dhoni, the legendary Indian cricketer, highlighting his journey from a small-town boy to becoming the captain of the Indian national cricket team and winning the World Cup.

  2. The Pursuit of Happyness

    Year Released: 2006

    Main Actors: Will Smith, Jaden Smith, Thandiwe Newton

    Description: Based on a true story, this film depicts the struggles of Chris Gardner, a single father who overcomes homelessness while striving to build a better future for himself and his son through unwavering determination and sheer grit.

  3. Home Alone

    Year Released: 1990

    Main Actors: Macaulay Culkin, Joe Pesci, Daniel Stern

    Description: This classic family comedy tells the story of Kevin McCallister, a young boy who is accidentally left behind when his family goes on vacation. Kevin must defend his home from two bumbling burglars using his wits and a series of ingenious traps.

  4. Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

    Year Released: 2013

    Main Actors: Ranbir Kapoor, Deepika Padukone, Aditya Roy Kapur, Kalki Koechlin

    Description: This Bollywood coming-of-age film explores the lives of four friends and their journey through love, friendship, and self-discovery, emphasizing the importance of pursuing one's passions and the balance between career and personal life.

  5. Monsters, Inc.

    Year Released: 2001

    Main Actors: John Goodman, Billy Crystal, Mary Gibbs

    Description: Set in a world where monsters generate their city's power by scaring children, this animated adventure follows two monster friends, Sulley and Mike, as they encounter a human child who changes their perspective on the human world.
